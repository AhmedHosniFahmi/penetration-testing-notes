## Working with Files and Directories 
- `cd` or `chdir `  (change dir)
- `dir` or `ls`     (list dir)
- `tree`            (tree dir)
- `md` or `mkdir`   (create dir)
- `rd` or `rmdir`   (remove dir) (add `\S` for recusrive)
